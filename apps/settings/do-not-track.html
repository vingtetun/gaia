Do Not Track

How does Do Not Track work?

  • When you turn on the Do Not Track feature, your device tells every website and app (as well as advertisers and other content providers) that you don’t want your behavior tracked.

    Turning on Do Not Track won’t affect your ability to log into websites, nor cause your device to forget your private information — such as the contents of shopping carts, location information, or log-in information.
